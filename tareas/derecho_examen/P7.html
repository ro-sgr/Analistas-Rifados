Problema 6

Si \( a \in \mathbb{R} \) tal que \( a<0 \), entonces \( \frac{1}{a}<0 \)

Demostración
$$ \begin{align*} a < 0 & \implies a \left( \frac{a}{a} \right) < 0 \\ & \implies (a)(a)\left( \frac{1}{a} \right) < 0 \\ & \implies \frac{1}{a} < \frac{0}{a} \\ & \implies \frac{1}{a} < 0 \end{align*} $$
